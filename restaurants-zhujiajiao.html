🌸 Zhujiajiao · Top 10 Must-Eat Restaurants

Water town farmhouse cuisine · 400-year-old culinary heritage · Nov 26

🐟#1

Ancient Bridge Family · 古橋人家•水上餐廳

朱家角古鎮店 · Dianping 4.5 · Riverside · Top Rated in Zhujiajiao
📍 朱家角古鎮景區內（放生橋附近，水上平台）| 🚇 Line 17 朱家角站，步行10分鐘 📞 （見大眾點評）| 🕐 10:30–21:00 💰 ¥77/人 | ⭐ Dianping 4.5 · 458+ 點評
🏆 What It's Famous For:

Zhujiajiao's most beloved riverside restaurant — dining on a floating deck with lantern light on the canal. The kitchen serves fresh river fish daily, caught each morning. Must-order: Salted River Shrimp (鹽水河蝦), Steamed Eel (招牌河鰻), Silver Fish Scrambled Eggs (銀魚炒蛋), Osmanthus Cake (桂花糕), Local Free-Range Chicken (本地雞).

🐟 Must-Order: Steamed River Eel + Salted Shrimp 🌊 Waterfront dining · Magic evening ⭐ Top-rated in Zhujiajiao
🦐#2

Fangsheng Bridge Restaurant · 放生橋菜館

朱家角鎮北大街172號 · Est. 1990s · Dianping 4.6 · #1 on Trip.com
📍 朱家角鎮北大街172號（放生橋旁）| 🚇 Line 17 朱家角站 📞 021-59246658 | 🕐 11:00–20:00 💰 ¥100/人 | ⭐ Dianping 4.6 · Trip.com #1 朱家角餐廳
🏆 What It's Famous For:

The go-to restaurant for Zhujiajiao locals since the 1990s — steps from the iconic Fangsheng Bridge. Water town home cooking at its best. Must-order: Braised Zha Meat (扎肉 — Zhujiajiao signature pork belly), Salted River Shrimp (鹽水河蝦), Steamed White Fish (清蒸白水魚), Eel Shred in Oil (響油鱔絲), Oil-Braised Jiaobai (油燜茭白).

🦐 Must-Order: Braised Zha Meat + River Shrimp 🌉 Iconic bridge view ⭐ #1 on Trip.com · Local favourite
🏮#3

Zhuangyuan Lou · 狀元樓

北大街（明清老街）· Est. 1800s · 200+ Year Heritage · Dianping 4.4
📍 朱家角古鎮北大街（明清老街核心）| 🚇 Line 17 朱家角站 📞 （見大眾點評）| 🕐 10:00–21:00 💰 ¥60–80/人 | ⭐ Dianping 4.4 · 200+ years old
🏆 What It's Famous For:

One of Zhujiajiao's oldest restaurants — the name means "Scholars' Hall," evoking the scholar-official tradition. The 200-year-old timber building with hand-hewn beams is itself a reason to visit. Must-order: Hand-Made Zongzi with Pulled Pork (手作粽子), Red-Cooked Pork (紅燒肉), River Fish Soup (河魚湯), Local Chicken (本地土雞).

🏮 Must-Order: Hand-Made Zongzi + Red-Cooked Pork 📸 Heritage building · 200+ years ⭐ One of oldest in Zhujiajiao
🏛️#4

Qingdai Shuzhai · 清代舒宅角里大院

朱家角古鎮 · 清代古宅改建 · Heritage Building Restaurant · Dianping 4.6
📍 朱家角古鎮內（北大街南端）| 🚇 Line 17 朱家角站 📞 （見大眾點評）| 🕐 11:00–20:30 💰 ¥90–120/人 | ⭐ Dianping 4.6 · 清代古宅
🏆 What It's Famous For:

Dining inside a restored Qing Dynasty courtyard home — original timber beams, stone floors, lantern light. One of the most atmospheric dining experiences in any ancient water town. Try the farmhouse-style set menus: Steamed River Fish (清蒸河鮮), Zha Meat with Glutinous Rice (扎肉糯米飯), Wild Vegetable Dumplings (野菜水餃).

🏛️ Must-Order: Set Menu (Heritage Experience) 📸 Qing Dynasty courtyard dining ⭐ Top atmosphere · Dianping 4.6
🔥#5

Jiu Tan Lao Zao · 酒罈老灶柴火飯

朱家角古鎮 · 柴火灶燒 · Dianping 4.5 · Authentic Farmhouse
📍 朱家角古鎮（近放生橋）| 🚇 Line 17 朱家角站 📞 （見大眾點評）| 🕐 10:30–20:30 💰 ¥65–85/人 | ⭐ Dianping 4.5
🏆 What It's Famous For:

Firewood-cooked meals over an open flame — the old farmhouse way. The smoky aroma infuses every dish. Must-order: Charcoal-Fired Red-Cooked Pork (柴火紅燒肉), Firewood Chicken Soup (柴火土雞湯), Smoked Fish (燻魚), Farmhouse Vegetables (农家青菜).

🔥 Must-Order: Charcoal Pork + Firewood Chicken Soup 🍚 Firewood cooking · Authentic ⭐ Popular · Arrive before 12pm
🍶#6

Cao Xi Ren Jia · 漕溪人家酒樓

朱家角古鎮 · Dianping 4.4 · Water Town Cuisine · Affordable
📍 朱家角鎮漕河街（古鎮北段）| 🚇 Line 17 朱家角站 📞 （見大眾點評）| 🕐 10:30–20:00 💰 ¥55–75/人 | ⭐ Dianping 4.4
🏆 What It's Famous For:

A local favourite tucked away in the quieter north canal area of the ancient town. Home-cooked Zhujiajiao dishes at approachable prices. Specialties: Braised Pork Belly with Taro (芋頭紅燒肉), River Shrimp with Leeks (韭菜河蝦), Crispy Rice Crust (鍋巴).

🍶 Must-Order: Braised Pork + River Shrimp 🏠 Local neighbourhood favourite 💰 Budget-friendly · ¥55–75
🥢#7

Hong De Lou · 弘德樓

朱家角古鎮 · Dianping 4.3 · Traditional Courtyard Restaurant
📍 朱家角古鎮景區內（課植園附近）| 🚇 Line 17 朱家角站 📞 （見大眾點評）| 🕐 10:00–20:30 💰 ¥65–85/人 | ⭐ Dianping 4.3
🏆 What It's Famous For:

A traditional courtyard restaurant offering authentic Zhujiajiao home cooking in a restored building. Known for generous portions and honest, hearty farmhouse flavour. Must-order: Zha Meat (扎肉), Steamed River Fish (清蒸河魚), Water Shield Soup (西湖蓴菜湯), Wontons in Broth (鮮肉餛飩).

🥢 Must-Order: Zha Meat + Steamed River Fish 🏠 Hearty home cooking · Generous portions ⭐ Traditional atmosphere
🌿#8

Forest Family · 森林人家

朱家角古鎮 · Dianping 4.4 · Garden Setting · Peaceful
📍 朱家角古鎮景區內（近課植園）| 🚇 Line 17 朱家角站 📞 （見大眾點評）| 🕐 10:00–21:00 💰 ¥70–90/人 | ⭐ Dianping 4.4
🏆 What It's Famous For:

A quiet garden restaurant set among bamboo and ancient trees — one of the few truly peaceful dining spots in the ancient town. The kitchen serves farm-to-table dishes with local seasonal ingredients. Must-order: Bamboo Shoots with River Shrimp (竹筍河蝦), Steamed Chicken (清蒸土雞), Wild Vegetable Frittata (野菜蛋).

🌿 Must-Order: Seasonal Set Menu 🌳 Garden setting · Peaceful escape ⭐ Farm-to-table · Seasonal
☕#9

Apo Tea House · 阿婆茶樓

北大街125號 · Est. 1990s · 100-Year-Old Tea House · Dianping 4.3
📍 朱家角鎮北大街125號（二樓，近放生橋）| 🚇 Line 17 朱家角站 📞 （見大眾點評）| 🕐 9:00–21:00 💰 ¥30–50/人 | ⭐ Dianping 4.3 · 100年曆史 · 2025 必吃榜
🏆 What It's Famous For:

The iconic second-floor tea house overlooking the canal — wooden stairs, old teacups, the smell of osmanthus tea and rice wine cakes. Zhujiajiao's most atmospheric traditional experience. Must-order: Grandma Tea (阿婆茶), Osmanthus Cake (桂花糕), Rice Wine (米酒), Boiled peanuts (水煮花生).

☕ Must-Order: Grandma Tea + Osmanthus Cake 🌸 Iconic canal view · 2nd floor terrace ⭐ #1 tea house experience in Zhujiajiao
🏨#10

Zhujiajiao Amber Resort · 朱家角安麓酒店·全日餐廳

朱家角古鎮 · Dianping 4.8 · Luxurious Resort Dining · 《周生如故》拍攝地
📍 青浦區朱家角鎮課植園路177號（安麓酒店內）| 🚇 Line 17 朱家角站，車程5分鐘 📞 021-3923 7777 | 🕐 6:30–22:00（住店客人優先） 💰 ¥320/人 | ⭐ Dianping 4.8 · Luxury Resort · 《周生如故》拍攝地
🏆 What It's Famous For:

Zhujiajiao's most luxurious dining experience — served at the Amber Resort, a converted 1920s estate. The setting is extraordinary: original Qing Dynasty architecture, lily ponds, scholars' gardens. The tasting menu changes with seasons. Must-order: Six-Treasure Tasting Set (六福鑒賞套餐), Slow-Braised Lamb (慢燉羊肉), Lotus Root in Osprey Sauce (糯米藕).

🏨 Must-Order: Tasting Menu · Reserve ahead 🌳 Resort setting · Historic estate ⭐ Dianping 4.8 · Best in class
