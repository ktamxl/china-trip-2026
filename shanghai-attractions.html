1

The Bund Night View

外滩 · Waitan · 黄浦区中山东一路

Shanghai's most iconic skyline. The 1.5 km waterfront promenade on both sides of the Huangpu River — 52 Art Deco buildings from the 1930s. The view at dusk, when the lights come on, is genuinely unforgettable.

⭐ Signature Night View 🌃 Best at Night 🚶 Free

2

Yu Garden & City God Temple

豫园 · 上海城隍庙 · 旧城区东北

Classical Ming Dynasty garden (built 1577) surrounded by the atmospheric old city. The adjacent City God Temple Market is perfect for souvenirs, street food, and appreciating traditional architecture. Allow 2–3 hours.

🏛️ Ming Dynasty 🌿 Garden 🍜 Street Food
🗺️ Amap → 📍 31.2242°N · 121.4855°E
3

French Concession · Anfu Road

法租界 · 安福路 · 徐汇区

Shanghai's most charming tree-lined neighbourhood. Anfu Road is the creative heart — boutique coffee shops, independent bookshops, fashion stores, and leafy villa-lined streets that feel a world away from the skyline.

☕ Boutique Cafés 🛍️ Shopping 🏡 Tree-Lined Streets
🗺️ Amap → 📍 31.2238°N · 121.4518°E
4

Shanghai Disney Resort

上海迪士尼度假区 · 浦东新区川沙新镇

China's first Disney resort — one of the world's largest and most technologically advanced. Features the largest Disney castle, a pirates-themed land, and the longest Disney parade route. Full day experience (8:30 AM – 9:30 PM).

🎢 Full Day 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family 🎠 Rides
🗺️ Amap → 📍 31.1414°N · 121.6670°E
5

Oriental Pearl Tower

东方明珠塔 · 浦东新区世纪大道1号

The symbol of modern Shanghai. 468 metres of steel and glass — the world's third-tallest TV tower. The observation decks at 259m and 351m give an unobstructed 360° view of the entire city. Open 9 AM – 9 PM daily.

⭐ Iconic Landmark 🏙️ 360° View 📸 Photo Spot
🗺️ Amap → 📍 31.2420°N · 121.4950°E
6

Shanghai Tower

上海中心大厦 · 浦东新区陆家嘴银城中路501号

The world's second-tallest building at 632 metres. The observation deck on the 118th floor (561m) is the highest in China. Next to the Oriental Pearl Tower and Shanghai World Financial Center — the three form the jaw-dropping Lujiazui skyline.

🏆 World's 2nd Tallest 🏙️ Lujiazui 📸 Best Skyline View
🗺️ Amap → 📍 31.2358°N · 121.5008°E
7

Xintiandi

新天地 · 黄浦区太仓路/兴业路

Shanghai's most stylish dining and entertainment district. The entire area is built around restored Shikumen stone-gate townhouses — 1920s architecture merged with modern interiors. Lined with international restaurants, cocktail bars, and boutique shops.

🥃 Nightlife 🍽️ Dining 🏛️ Shikumen Architecture
🗺️ Amap → 📍 31.2238°N · 121.4629°E
8

Jing'an Temple

靜安寺 · 靜安區南京西路1686號

One of the oldest Buddhist temples in Shanghai — originally built in 220 AD. Despite being surrounded by the city's most upscale shopping district (West Nanjing Road), the temple retains its spiritual atmosphere with ancient pagodas and incense-filled halls.

🛕 Temple 🏛️ Ancient (220 AD) 🪷 Incense
🗺️ Amap → 📍 31.2250°N · 121.4425°E
9

Nanjing Road Pedestrian Street

南京路步行街 · 黄浦区南京东路558号

The world's busiest shopping street — "China's First Commercial Street." The 1.3 km fully pedestrianised stretch runs from the Bund to People's Square. Hundreds of international brands, department stores, and local specialties line both sides.

🛍️ Shopping 🏮 Pedestrian Street 🚶 Free
🗺️ Amap → 📍 31.2362°N · 121.4809°E

Louis Vuitton Visionary Journeys

路易威登「非凡之旅」限时展 · 靜安區南京西路789號興業太古匯

A landmark immersive exhibition by Louis Vuitton, centred on the "The Louis" — the world's only ship-shaped concept space. Four themed zones exploring travel, craft, fashion, and innovation. Free entry with reservation. Located at Pulse 808 mall.

👜 Luxury Exhibition 🎨 Art & Fashion 📸 Instagram Spot
