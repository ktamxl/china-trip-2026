🌆 Shanghai · Top 10 Must-Eat Restaurants

Based on 2025 Dianping Must-Eat List + Tripadvisor + Michelin Guide · Nov 23–27

🥟#1

Nanxiang Steamed Bun Shop · 南翔饅頭店

豫園店 · Est. 1900 · China's Most Iconic Xiaolongbao
📍 黃浦區豫園路87號（豫園商城九曲橋旁）| 🚇 Line 10/14 豫園站，步行5分鐘 📞 021-63554206 | 🕐 7:30–20:00（地庫1層外賣；2-3層堂食） 💰 ¥91/人 | ⭐ 4.6 Dianping | 🏆 2025 必吃榜 · Tripadvisor #1 城隍廟
🏆 What It's Famous For:

The pinnacle of Shanghai xiaolongbao — thin-skinned, broth-stuffed parcels you drink through a straw. Queues snake out daily. Must-order: Fresh Pork Xiaolongbao (鮮肉小籠), Crab Roe Xiaolongbao (蟹黃小籠), Giant Soup Dumpling (蟹黃灌湯包). The original and still the best since 1900.

🥟 Must-Order: Xiaolongbao ⏱️ Queue expected · Arrive before 10am ⭐ #1 Dianping · 125 years old
🥢#2

Green Wave Corridor · 綠波廊

豫園路店 · Est. 1979 · Michelin Guide · Historic Shanghai Cuisine
📍 黃浦區豫園路115號（九曲橋畔，城隍廟對面）| 🚇 Line 10/14 豫園站 📞 021-63280602 | 🕐 午市 11:00–14:00 · 晚市 17:00–20:30 💰 ¥165/人 | ⭐ Michelin Guide · Dianping 4.5
🏆 What It's Famous For:

A legendary Shanghai dining room steps from the ancient Nine-Cornered Bridge. Housed in a building that dates back to 1559. The Michelin Guide says: "Signature dishes include Radish Shred Pastry (蘿蔔絲酥餅), Osmanthus Rice Cake (桂花拉糕), Eyebrow Prawn Wontons (眉毛酥蝦仁春卷), Crab Roe Xiaolongbao (蟹粉小籠包)." Don't miss them all.

🥢 Must-Order: Radish Shred Pastry + Osmanthus Cake 🏛️ Built 1559 · Historic ⭐ Michelin Guide
🍜#3

Jia Jia Tang Bao · 佳家湯包

黃河路店 · Est. 1990s · Top-Rated Soup Dumplings · Dianping 4.8
📍 黃浦區黃河路127號（南京西路步行街近地鐵）| 🚇 Line 1/2/8 南京西路站 📞 021-63278868 | 🕐 6:30–20:30 💰 ¥70/人 | ⭐ Dianping 4.8 · 2025 必吃榜
🏆 What It's Famous For:

Shanghai locals' secret xiaolongbao spot — fewer tourists than Nanxiang, same legendary quality. The soup broth is rich and gelatinous; the skin is delicate yet holds together. Must-order: Pure Crab Roe Soup Dumpling (純蟹黃湯包), Fresh Pork Soup Dumpling (鮮肉湯包). Many say the crab roe version rivals the best in the world.

🍜 Must-Order: Pure Crab Roe Tangbao 🕕 Opens 6:30am · Early bird friendly ⭐ Dianping 4.8 · Local favourite
🥟#4

Xiao Yang Sheng Jian · 小楊生煎

黃河路店 · Est. 1992 · Shanghai's #1 Shengjianbao
📍 黃浦區黃河路41號（近南京西路步行街）| 🚇 Line 1/2 南京西路站 📞 021-63279777 | 🕐 7:00–22:00 💰 ¥30–50/人 | ⭐ Dianping 4.5 · 上海名點 · 2025 必吃榜
🏆 What It's Famous For:

Shanghai's most famous pan-fried pork bun (生煎包). Thin skin, copious soup inside, sesame-crusted bottom fried to a crisp. Dozens of branches citywide — every meal feels like the original. Must-order: Pork Shengjianbao (鮮肉生煎), Beef + Glass Noodle Soup (牛肉粉絲湯). A must for any Shanghai visitor.

🥟 Must-Order: Pan-Fried Pork Bun + Beef Soup 💰 Budget-friendly · ¥30–50 ⭐ 30+ branches citywide
🥘#5

San Ma Lu · 三瑪璐酒樓

漢口路店 · Est. 1996 · 2025 Dianping Must-Eat List
📍 黃浦區漢口路413號（南京東路商圈，近山西南路）| 🚇 Line 2/10 南京東路站 📞 021-63517909 | 🕐 9:00–21:30 💰 ¥80–100/人 | ⭐ Dianping 2025 必吃榜 · 28 years old
🏆 What It's Famous For:

A neighbourhood Benbang (本幫) institution since 1996. The name comes from the old Shanghai street name "三瑪璐" (Three Ma Road). Renowned for authentic Shanghai home cooking — the kind locals grew up on. Must-order: Wasabi Shrimp (芥末蝦仁), Braised Pork Liver (醬爆豬肝), Dry Pot Bullfrog (干鍋牛蛙), Pan-Fried Pork Chops (上海小排).

🥘 Must-Order: Wasabi Shrimp + Braised Pork Liver 🏠 29-year neighbourhood favourite ⭐ 2025 Dianping Must-Eat
🍖#6

Old Ji Tang Benbang · 老吉堂上海本幫菜

外灘店 · Est. 2010s · Award-Winning Shanghai Cuisine · Dianping 4.6
📍 黃浦區北京東路118號（近外灘）| 🚇 Line 2/10 南京東路站 📞 021-6323 1818 | 🕐 11:00–14:00 · 17:00–21:30 💰 ¥218/人 | ⭐ Dianping 4.6
🏆 What It's Famous For:

Award-winning Shanghainese Benbang cuisine steps from the Bund. The restaurant brings traditional recipes into an elegant setting. Must-order: Red-Cooked Pork (紅燒肉), Drunken Chicken (醉雞), Stir-Fried Shrimp with Longjing Tea (龍井蝦仁), Crab Roe Tofu (蟹粉豆腐).

🍖 Must-Order: Red-Cooked Pork + Drunken Chicken 🌃 Near the Bund · Evening dinner ideal ⭐ Award-winning Benbang
🦐#7

Yue Xiang Qun · 粵·向群飯店

上海浦東店 · Est. 1970s · Dianping 4.7 · Authentic Cantonese
📍 浦東新區浦東南路1285號（近世紀大道）| 🚇 Line 2 上海科技館站 📞 021-5833 8888 | 🕐 11:00–14:30 · 17:00–21:30 💰 ¥150/人 | ⭐ Dianping 4.7 · 2025 必吃榜
🏆 What It's Famous For:

One of Shanghai's most beloved Cantonese restaurants — a local institution since the 1970s. Renowned for wok-hei (鍋氣) — that elusive breath of the wok. Must-order: Salted Chicken (鹽焗雞), Steamed Fish with Ginger & Scallion (清蒸石斑), Crispy Pork Belly (脆皮燒肉), Century Egg with Pork Congee (皮蛋瘦肉粥).

🦐 Must-Order: Salted Chicken + Steamed Fish 🥘 Authentic Cantonese · wok-hei ⭐ 50+ years old
🥧#8

Guangming Village · 光明邨

黃浦區南京西路店 · Est. 1949 · Shanghai Heritage Cake Shop
📍 黃浦區南京西路1172號（近黃河路）| 🚇 Line 1/2/8 南京西路站 📞 021-6322 3998 | 🕐 8:00–20:00 💰 ¥40–60/人 | ⭐ Dianping 4.4 · 76 years old
🏆 What It's Famous For:

Shanghai's most beloved old-school bakery since 1949 — the queue snakes around the block every weekend for their signature鲜肉月饼 (savoury pork mooncake) and各式本幫糕點. No-frills, no atmosphere, perfect food. Must-order: Pork Mooncake (鮮肉月餅), Walnut Tofu (核桃豆腐), Assorted Pastries (各款糕點).

🥧 Must-Order: Pork Mooncake + Assorted Pastries 🏠 76-year heritage bakery · Queue expected ⭐ Local institution
🥟#9

Da Hu Chun · 大壺春

雲南路店 · Est. 1927 · Shanghai's #2 Shengjianbao · Dianping 4.5
📍 黃浦區雲南路60號（近南京西路）| 🚇 Line 8 曲阜路站 📞 021-6328 0293 | 🕐 6:30–19:00 💰 ¥30–50/人 | ⭐ Dianping 4.5 · 2025 必吃榜 · 98 years old
🏆 What It's Famous For:

Shanghai's oldest surviving shengjianbao (pan-fried bun) specialist — founded in 1927. Unlike the薄皮派 xiaolongbao at Nanxiang, Da Hu Chun uses a thick发酵面皮 — fluffy, doughy, and utterly satisfying. The opposite school of pan-fried buns. Must-order: Shengjianbao (生煎包), Beef Soup (牛肉湯).

🥟 Must-Order: Thick-Skin Shengjianbao 🕕 Opens 6:30am · Breakfast perfect ⭐ 98 years old · Original recipe
🍰#10

Mr. Choi Patisserie · 蔡嘉甜品

豫園店 · Est. 2010s · Shanghai's #1 French Pastry · Dianping 4.8
📍 黃浦區豫園百貨天裕樓對面（豫園商圈）| 🚇 Line 10/14 豫園站 📞 （見大眾點評）| 🕐 10:00–22:00 💰 ¥50/人 | ⭐ Dianping 4.8+ · Top-rated in Shanghai
🏆 What It's Famous For:

Shanghai's most celebrated French patisserie — their legendary Napoleon (拿破侖) has won devoted fans citywide. Layer upon layer of ultra-crisp mille-feuille with fresh cream — light, never cloying. Also exceptional: seasonal fruit tarts, cream puffs, chocolate fondants. The ideal afternoon stop after exploring Yuyuan Garden.

🍰 Must-Order: Napoleon + Seasonal Tarts 🌸 Perfect after Yuyuan Garden ⭐ Shanghai's #1 Pastry Shop
