After a packed itinerary of sightseeing and eating, a Shanghai spa day is the perfect reset. These three venues represent three very different approaches to luxury wellness — from an all-out 24-hour lifestyle club with hot springs and lobster buffet, to a Forbes five-star hotel spa with Bund views.

🥇 #1 曲水蘭亭 (Qushui Lanting)

📍 闵行区虹梅路3337号 · Hongqiao Town, Minhang · 虹橋商圈

🏆 #1

曲水蘭亭 — "Hermes of Shanghai Spas"

上海頂級休閒生活會所 · 24小時 · 虹橋旗艦店

The most famous luxury spa in Shanghai — widely considered the gold standard and a genuine bucket-list experience. Known online as the "Hermes of bathhouses," Qushui Lanting is an all-in-one lifestyle destination where guests truly spend an entire day (or night). Step inside and the city outside simply stops.

🥈 #2 楊SPACE溫泉生活 (Yang Space Hot Spring Life)

📍 浦东新区浦电路57号 · Pudong · 陸家嘴商圈

♨️ #2

楊SPACE — 陆家嘴天然温泉

上海性價比最高溫泉會所 · 浦東店 · 24小時

A viral hit with expats and younger travellers — real natural hot spring water (not heated tap water) with an impressive all-you-can-eat spread. The Pudong (Lujiazui) location is the most popular branch. The vibe is contemporary and social, with co-ed lounge areas, professional massage services, and free-flow Haagen-Dazs ice cream.

⭐ Signature Features

  • ♨️ 天然溫泉 — real spring water from underground springs
  • 🍦 哈根達斯無限 — free-flow Haagen-Dazs + Lavazza coffee
  • 🥤 高級飲品 — premium beverages & fresh fruit juices
  • 💆 專業按摩 — aromatherapy, foot reflexology, hot stone
  • 🛋️ 睡眠艙 — nap pods for daytime rest
  • 🍜 餐廳 — hot pot, noodles, dim sum available
  • 24/7 — great for overnight stays

🥉 #3 半島酒店水療 (The Peninsula Spa)

📍 外滩32號 · The Peninsula Shanghai · 32/F

🏅 #3

半島水療 — Forbes五星 · 外灘全景

福布斯旅遊指南五星水療 · 經典奢華 · 適合商務與夫妻

The Peninsula Spa is the only hotel spa in Shanghai to hold a Forbes Travel Guide Five-Star rating — a distinction it has retained year after year. Located on the 32nd floor of The Peninsula, it offers sweeping Bund and Huangpu River views from the treatment suites. Intimate, refined, and world-class service.

⭐ Signature Features
