Shenzhen Talent Park

人才公園 · 南山区后海片区

Shenzhen's most scenic lake-side park, designed around a ring of water and a crescent moon bridge. Walk the lakeside boardwalk at sunset — the CBD skyline reflected on the water creates one of Shenzhen's most photographed scenes. Free entry, open 6 AM – 11 PM.

🌅 Sunset View 🌿 Lakeside 📸 Free

2

Shenzhen Bay Park

深圳灣公園 · 南山区望海路

A 13-kilometre seaside urban park along Shenzhen Bay. One of the city's most beloved green spaces — palm-lined promenades, ocean breezes, and views across the bay to Hong Kong. The sunset here is spectacular. Best accessed from Houhai section (South). Free entry.

🌊 Seaside 🚴 13 km Promenade 🌅 Free
🗺️ Amap → 📍 22.5034°N · 113.9460°E
3

Safari Park Shenzhen

野生動物園 · 南山区西丽湖东侧 4065号

China's first zoo with uncaged animals — visitors drive through savanna-style enclosures. Home to over 300 species including giant pandas, golden snub-nosed monkeys, and rare white tigers. 640,000 sqm of natural landscape. One of China's top-rated family attractions. Allow a full day.

🐼 Giant Pandas 🚗 Drive-Through Safari 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family
🗺️ Amap → 📍 22.5839°N · 113.9309°E
4

OCT Harbour

歡樂港灣 · 南山区望海路2008号

Shenzhen's most beloved waterfront entertainment district. A lakeside paradise with boutique shops, international restaurants, a huge indoor theme park, and a musical fountain. The waterfront at night, lit up, is genuinely magical — ranked #5 family attraction in Guangdong. Free to walk around.

🎆 Waterfront 🥃 Nightlife 🛍️ Shopping
🗺️ Amap → 📍 22.4997°N · 113.9385°E
5

Shenzhen Happy Valley

歡樂谷 · 南山区华侨城

The original Happy Valley theme park — the连锁 brand's flagship. Nine themed zones with over 100 rides including roller coasters, water rides, and live performances. 322,000 sqm of family fun. One of Guangdong's top theme parks. Allow a full day.

🎢 Roller Coasters 💧 Water Rides 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family
🗺️ Amap → 📍 22.5424°N · 113.9367°E
6

Sea World

海上世界 · 南山区蛇口望海路1128号

The iconic seafront district in Shekou, built around the retired Minghua — a luxury cruise ship converted into a landmark. A bustling waterfront lined with bars, restaurants, and shops. Perfect for an evening stroll, dinner by the water, and people-watching in one of Shenzhen's most cosmopolitan corners.

🚢 Iconic Landmark 🌊 Waterfront 🥃 Evening Walk
🗺️ Amap → 📍 22.4830°N · 113.9120°E
7

Gankeng Hakka Town

甘坑客家小镇 · 龙岗区甘坑社区甘李路18号

A beautifully preserved Hakka village turned cultural tourism destination. The town has retained its traditional Hakka architecture —古老的围龙屋, stone-paved lanes, and canals. Try authentic Hakka cuisine (盐焗鸡, 客家酿豆腐) and browse handcraft workshops. Ranked #5 spring outing destination in Guangdong.

🏘️ Hakka Heritage 🍜 Hakka Cuisine 📸 Traditional Architecture
🗺️ Amap → 📍 22.6568°N · 114.1005°E
8

OCT Harbour Waterfront

歡樂港灣濱海區 · 同上

The waterfront extension of OCT Harbour — a stunning lakeside promenade with the Bay Glory Ferris Wheel as its centrepiece. The best evening photography spot in Nanshan: the ferris wheel reflected in the lake at dusk, with Hong Kong visible across the water. Free entry. Best at sunset.

🎡 Best Sunset Spot 📸 Ferris Wheel 🌅 Free
🗺️ Amap → 📍 22.4997°N · 113.9385°E (same as OCT Harbour)
9

Shenzhen Bay Cultural Plaza

深圳灣文化廣場 · 南山区科苑南路2516號

Shenzhen's newest landmark — eight giant stone-like pavilions designed by MAD Architects (Ma Yansong) that resemble oversized AirPods emerging from the ground. Opened late 2025. A major waterfront cultural space for design and science exhibitions. Metro Line 13 to Talent Park Station, Exit B1.

🏛️ New Landmark 🎨 Design Museum 📸 Architecture
🗺️ Amap → 📍 22.5060°N · 113.9380°E
10

Shenzhen Science Life Hall

深圳科学生活馆 · 深圳灣文化廣場內

Located within the Shenzhen Bay Cultural Plaza complex. An interactive science education centre exploring innovation and future living concepts. The space hosts rotating exhibitions on technology, design, and everyday science. Check hours before visiting — currently under renovation for some zones.

🔬 Science 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family 🎓 Interactive
🗺️ Amap → 📍 22.5060°N · 113.9380°E (same plaza)
11

Shenzhen Bay MixC

深圳灣萬象城 · 南山区科苑南路2888号

Shenzhen's most prestigious bay-front luxury shopping centre. High-end international brands, a curated food hall, and waterfront views in one space. Connected to Houhai Metro Station (Lines 9/11). The exterior at night, with the tower lit up, makes for great photos from the boardwalk.

🛍️ Luxury Shopping 🍽️ Dining 🌅 Bay Views
🗺️ Amap → 📍 22.5182°N · 113.9368°E

Shenzhen Library North Hall

深圳圖書館北館 · 龍華區騰龍路30號

Shenzhen's newest major cultural institution — a stunning white building in Longhua District designed to hold 8 million books with 2,500 reading seats. The counterpart to the main library in Futian. One of the largest public library buildings in Asia. Air-conditioned, peaceful, and free. Great for a quiet afternoon or an architectural visit.

📚 Architecture 📖 Free ☕ Quiet Afternoon
