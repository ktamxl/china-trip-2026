1START
North · Entry Point

遊客中心

Tourist Center · 课植园路 555号

Start your walk here. Grab a map, store luggage, and use the restroom before heading in. Free entry to the ancient town streets.

📍 Start Here 🚻 Restroom 🅿️ Parking
2

課植園

Kezhi Garden · 課植園路

Zhujiajiao's crown jewel. A private Qing Dynasty garden built by a magistrate — named after the motto "study farming, plant trees." The finest classical garden in the water town. Optional ticket (¥40).

🌿 Garden 🏛️ Heritage 📸 Photo Spot
3

和心園

Hexin Garden · 西湖街

A traditional garden perfect for a break. Try on Hanfu costume rental, enjoy a pot of local tea, or simply rest your feet before continuing south.

🍵 Tea House 👘 Hanfu Rental
4

永安橋

Yong'an Bridge · 永安桥

The iconic stone arch bridge. One of the most photographed spots in Zhujiajiao — classic water town framing with boats and reflections below. Best shot: from either bank looking toward the bridge.

📸 Signature Shot 🌉 Bridge 🚣 Boat View
5

阿婆茶樓

Apo Tea House · 北大街 125号

A 100-year-old tea house on the second floor overlooking the river. Famous for "阿婆茶" (Grandma Tea) — local tea with rice wine and osmanthus cakes. Steep wooden stairs, charming atmosphere.

☕ Must-Do 🧁 Local Snack 📸 Iconic View
6

放生橋

Fangsheng Bridge · 放生桥

The tallest and longest stone bridge in Zhujiajiao. Built in 1625 (Ming Dynasty). The landmark of the ancient town — 5 vaulted arches, commanding views up and down the漕港河. Best at sunrise or just before sunset.

⭐ Signature Shot 🌉 Ming Dynasty 📸 Golden Hour
7

北大街

North Street · 北大街

The 1,000-meter Ming Dynasty shopping street — one of the "nine long streets" of Zhujiajiao. Lined with作坊 shops, snack stalls (try the 粽子 rice dumplings), and craft stores. Narrow and lively.

🍜 Street Food 🎁 Souvenirs 🏮 Old Street
8

鶴龍美術館

Helong Art Museum · 北大街 168号

A private art museum showcasing Chinese calligraphy, ink paintings, and traditional culture exhibitions. Air-conditioned and quiet — a peaceful stop after the busy street. Free or small donation.

🎨 Art 🖌️ Calligraphy
9

泰安橋

Tai'an Bridge · 泰安桥

One of the oldest bridges in Zhujiajiao. The stone arch creates a perfect natural frame for water reflections. The best reflection photography spot in the ancient town — aim for calm morning or evening.

📸 Best Reflection Shot 🌉 Oldest Bridge
10

圓津禪院

Yuanjin Zen Temple · 圆津禅院

A Qing Dynasty temple floating over the water — one of the most atmospheric spots in Zhujiajiao. Beautiful Buddhist art inside and serene waterside corridors. Small temple entry fee (¥10).

🛕 Temple 🪷 Buddhist Art 📸 Scenic
11

城隍廟

City God Temple · 城隍庙

The oldest and most revered temple in Zhujiajiao. Built during the Song Dynasty, reconstructed in the Qing. The stone courtyard and carved beams are extraordinary. Atmospheric during festivals.

🏛️ Ancient 🛕 Temple 📸 Architecture
12END
South · Final Stop

大清郵局

Great Qing Post Office · 漕河街 187号

Zhujiajiao's original Qing Dynasty post office (1896). Step inside to buy postcards and stamp your own custom Zhujiajiao postmark — a perfect keepsake to send to friends and family back home.

📮 Send a Postcard! 🏛️ Heritage 📸 Historical
