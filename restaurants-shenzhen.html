🥩#1

Iconic Korean BBQ chain born in Shenyang 1997. Traditional Northern Korean clay pot charcoal grilling using菊花炭. 28 types of house-marinated meats. Must-order: Premium Beef Set (優質牛肉拼盤), Marinated Pork Neck (醃製五花肉), 老太太特選牛肉. Staff assist with grilling on request.

🥩 Must-Order: Premium Beef Set + Marinated Pork🔥 Northern Korean charcoal tradition⭐ #1 BBQ in Nanshan

Guangzhou's beloved century-old dim sum institution — handcrafted to order, fresh from the kitchen. Must-order: Prawn Dumpling King (招牌蝦餃皇), Crispy Oil Stick (招牌靚油條), White Curry Fish Balls (咖喱魚蛋), Milky Custard Bun (椰汁鳳凰流沙包).

Tao Tao Ju · 陶陶居

深圳灣萬象城店 · Est. 1880 Guangzhou · 145-Year Cantonese Legend
📍 南山區深圳灣萬象城L3層（後海站E口直達）| 🚇 Line 2/11 後海站📞 0755-8652 1880 | 🕐 10:00–21:30💰 ¥126/人 | ⭐ Dianping 4.7 · 145 years old
🏆 What It's Famous For:

Guangzhou's oldest restaurant brand, founded in 1880. The legendary Roast Goose (燒鵝) — crispy skin, juicy meat, plum sauce. Also: Steamed Scallop (粉絲蒸扇貝), Braised Abalone (鮑魚扒鴨掌), Egg Tarts (蛋撻).

🦢 Must-Order: Roast Goose + Steamed Scallop⭐ 145 years of Cantonese mastery🏛️ Historic Guangzhou institution
